Sure, here are 15 breakfast recipe ideas:
Scrambled eggs with toast: Whisk eggs with milk, salt, and pepper, and cook in a non-stick skillet until fluffy. Serve with toast and butter.
Overnight oats: Combine rolled oats, milk, Greek yogurt, honey, and vanilla extract in a jar and refrigerate overnight. Top with fresh fruit and nuts in the morning.
Avocado toast: Mash avocado with lime juice, salt, and pepper and spread on toast. Top with a fried egg or cherry tomatoes.
Breakfast burrito: Scramble eggs with sausage, peppers, onions, and cheese, and wrap in a tortilla. Serve with salsa and sour cream.
Pancakes with fruit: Mix pancake batter and add in blueberries or sliced bananas. Cook on a griddle and serve with maple syrup.
Breakfast sandwich: Fry an egg and place it on a toasted English muffin with bacon, cheese, and arugula.
Yogurt parfait: Layer Greek yogurt with granola and fresh berries or sliced bananas.
Breakfast quesadilla: Fill a tortilla with scrambled eggs, ham, and cheese, and cook in a non-stick skillet until cheese is melted.
Smoothie bowl: Blend frozen fruit, Greek yogurt, and almond milk until smooth and thick. Top with granola and fresh fruit.
Shakshuka: Simmer eggs in a spicy tomato sauce with onions, bell peppers, and cumin. Serve with crusty bread.
Quiche: Mix eggs, milk, cheese, and cooked bacon or vegetables and pour into a pie crust. Bake until golden and set.
Belgian waffles: Mix waffle batter and cook in a Belgian waffle iron. Serve with whipped cream and fresh berries.
Huevos rancheros: Fry eggs and place on top of tortillas with refried beans, salsa, and avocado slices.
Bagel with cream cheese: Toast a bagel and spread with cream cheese. Top with smoked salmon and capers.
Frittata: Whisk eggs with cheese, vegetables, and herbs, and cook in a non-stick skillet until set. Cut into wedges and serve with toast.

Sure, here are 15 lunch recipe ideas:
Grilled cheese and tomato soup: Grill cheese between two slices of bread and serve with a bowl of tomato soup.
Tuna salad sandwich: Mix canned tuna with mayonnaise, diced celery, and pickles. Serve on bread with lettuce and tomato.
Caesar salad: Toss romaine lettuce with Caesar dressing, croutons, and Parmesan cheese. Add grilled chicken for protein.
Chicken quesadilla: Fill a tortilla with shredded chicken, cheese, and vegetables, and cook in a non-stick skillet until cheese is melted.
Club sandwich: Layer sliced turkey, ham, bacon, lettuce, and tomato on toasted bread with mayonnaise.
Caprese salad: Layer sliced tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and basil. Drizzle with balsamic vinegar and olive oil.
Chickpea salad: Mix chickpeas with diced vegetables, feta cheese, and a lemon vinaigrette.
Baked potato: Top a baked potato with shredded cheese, sour cream, and chives. Add bacon or chili for extra flavor.
Hummus and veggie wrap: Spread hummus on a tortilla and add sliced vegetables like cucumber, carrot, and bell pepper. Roll up and slice.
Falafel wrap: Fill a pita with falafel, lettuce, tomato, and tzatziki sauce.
Pasta salad: Mix cooked pasta with chopped vegetables, olives, feta cheese, and Italian dressing.
BLT sandwich: Layer bacon, lettuce, and tomato on bread with mayonnaise.
Veggie burger: Grill a veggie patty and serve on a bun with lettuce, tomato, and avocado.
Sushi roll: Fill sushi rice with avocado, cucumber, and cooked shrimp or crab. Serve with soy sauce and wasabi.
Greek salad: Toss lettuce with feta cheese, olives, red onion, and a lemon vinaigrette. Add grilled chicken or shrimp for protein.
